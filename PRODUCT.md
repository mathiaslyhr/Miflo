# Miflo — product brief (marketing site)

## What it is
Miflo is an iOS party game for football fans. Friends join a room with a 4-letter
code and play short, fast multiplayer games together: **Football Quiz** (trivia with a
speed bonus), **Odd One Out** (spot the player who breaks the pattern), and **Missing
XI** (name the missing player from an iconic line-up). No accounts, no sign-up — open
the app, pick a nickname, share a code.

This site (miflo.dk) is the front door: it tells people what Miflo is, sends them to
download it, hosts the privacy policy, and collects feedback.

## Audience
Football fans aged ~16–35 who play with friends in person or over a call. They're
competitive, quick, and already argue about football trivia. The site should feel like
matchday energy, not a corporate SaaS page.

## Voice
Playful, competitive, fast. Confident and a little cheeky, the way mates talk during a
game. Plain football language, no marketing buzzwords.

## Register
Brand (design IS the product). Committed dark palette; the design should feel like the
app it's selling.

## Brand system (inherited from the app — identity preservation, do not reinvent)
- Background `#000000`, surface `#1C1C1C`, divider hairlines.
- Accent `#6260F6` (electric purple), accent-ink `#E4E4FD`.
- Text `#FFFFFF` / muted `#9F9F9F`. Success `#32C36C`, error `#F0544A`.
- Font **Satoshi** (Regular 400 / Medium 500), bundled locally.
- Radii: cards 16px, buttons 14px, pills 999px.

## Primary actions
1. Get the app (TestFlight beta now → App Store when live).
2. Read the privacy policy (Apple requirement; linked from the app too).
3. Send feedback / questions / ideas (writes to the same Supabase backend as the app).
